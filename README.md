HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition Analysis
📖 About the Project

The HR Analytics Dashboard is a Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, and organizational trends. The dashboard transforms raw HR data into actionable insights, enabling HR teams and business leaders to make informed decisions that improve employee retention and workforce planning.

The analysis covers 1,417 employees, including 1,186 active employees and 231 employees who left the organization, resulting in an overall attrition rate of 16.3%.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze employee attrition across departments, salary groups, and job roles.
Identify workforce demographics and employee distribution.
Evaluate job satisfaction and experience trends.
Support HR decision-making with interactive visualizations.
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Dashboard Development & Visualization
Excel / CSV – Dataset
Power Query – Data Cleaning & Transformation
DAX – KPI Measures & Calculations
📊 Key Dashboard Metrics
👥 Total Employees: 1,417
✅ Active Employees: 1,186
❌ Attrition Count: 231
📉 Attrition Rate: 16.3%
🎂 Average Age: 36.94 Years
💼 Average Experience: 7.04 Years
📈 Key Insights
The organization has an overall 16.3% employee attrition rate.
Administration recorded the highest employee count, while Finance had the lowest.
Employees earning ₹6–10 LPA represented the largest workforce segment.
The 26–35 years age group accounted for the highest number of employees.
Male employees (63.2%) outnumbered female employees (36.8%).
Job role and job satisfaction analysis identified roles with comparatively higher employee turnover.
Experience and tenure analysis highlighted that attrition is highest during the early years of employment.
💼 Business Impact
Identifies departments with higher employee turnover.
Supports employee retention and workforce planning.
Helps HR teams monitor salary and job satisfaction trends.
Enables data-driven HR strategies through interactive dashboards.

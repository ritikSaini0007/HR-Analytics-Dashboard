# HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📖 About the Project

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is a Power BI project that analyzes employee attrition, workforce demographics, and HR performance metrics. The dashboard converts raw HR data into meaningful insights, helping organizations monitor employee turnover, identify workforce trends, and make data-driven HR decisions.

The analysis includes **1,417 employees**, with **1,186 active employees** and **231 attritions**, resulting in an overall **16.3% attrition rate**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across departments, salary groups, and job roles.
- Understand workforce demographics and employee distribution.
- Identify factors contributing to employee turnover.
- Support HR decision-making through interactive dashboards.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI**
- **Power Query**
- **DAX**
- **Excel / CSV Dataset**

---

## 📊 Key Dashboard Metrics

- 👥 Total Employees: **1,417**
- ✅ Active Employees: **1,186**
- ❌ Attrition Count: **231**
- 📉 Attrition Rate: **16.3%**
- 🎂 Average Age: **36.94 Years**
- 💼 Average Experience: **7.04 Years**

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Analyzed **1,417 employee records** with an overall **16.3% attrition rate**.
- **Administration** had the highest employee count, while **Finance** had the lowest.
- Employees aged **26–35 years** formed the largest workforce segment.
- **Male employees (63.2%)** outnumbered **Female employees (36.8%)**.
- Department-wise, salary-wise, and job role analysis highlighted key attrition trends.
- Experience analysis showed higher attrition during the early years of employment.

---

## 💼 Business Impact

- Identified departments with higher employee attrition.
- Improved workforce planning and retention strategies.
- Enabled HR teams to monitor employee demographics and satisfaction.
- Supported data-driven HR decision-making with interactive visualizations.
